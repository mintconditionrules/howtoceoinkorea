About

Learn More

HowtoCEOinKorea is a guide for foreign founders and CEOs looking to establish, fund, and scale companies in the Korean market.

We provide practical, actionable information on:

  • Founding and incorporating a company in Korea
  • Visa routes and residency requirements for founders
  • Korean startup ecosystem and policy landscape
  • Government funding, grants, and R&D budgets
  • Market trends and business recommendations

All content is curated and written by a certified business advisor with direct experience supporting foreign entrepreneurs in Korea.

Data Source

All articles and information published on this site are sourced from:

  • Primary sources: Official Korean government agencies (Ministry of SMEs and Startups, National Tax Service, Immigration Service)
  • Public data: Published economic reports, policy announcements, and statistical data from KOSIS (Korean Statistical Information Service)
  • Expert knowledge: Direct experience working with foreign founders and certified business advisory background
  • Industry research: Third-party reports on Korean startup trends and policy comparisons

Information is current as of the publication date listed on each article. Korean regulations and policies change frequently—always verify directly with official sources before making business decisions.
